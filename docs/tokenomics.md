## Token Economics

### Supply Mechanics
- **Minting**: Controlled by DAO after initial distribution
- **Burning**: Automatic burn on NFT minting and redemptions
- **Target**: Deflationary after Year 3

### Price Stability
- Treasury can intervene during extreme volatility
- Liquidity locked for minimum 1 year
- Gradual unlock schedule prevents dumping

### Revenue Model
Coffee shops pay platform fee in BREWFI:
- 2% transaction fee on rewards distributed
- 50% burned, 50% to treasury
- Creates buy pressure and deflationary effect
